Hi! I'm Anuj

I am a researcher in Quantum Information at University College London. I am currently working on minimising copies needed for quantum state tomography with computational techniques.

I previously worked on quantifying the latency of multi-party quantum communication using model-free reinforcement learning (see QAMEL).

I received my MSc in Quantum Technologies from University College London in 2023 and where I wrote my dissertation on the usage of recurrent networks for quantifying von Neumann entropy of random pure quantum states with Sougato Bose.

I did my undergraduate in Physics and graduated in 2022 with my dissertation on using computational techniques to determine properties of accreting binary systems using astronomical data.
